Components required for ESP32 Smart Plant Care Device:

1. ESP32 DEVKIT V1
2. OLED Display 128x64 px (I2C)
3. IR Distance Sensor (5 required, 4 for parking slots and 1 for gate)
4. SG90 Servo Motor (To be used as gate)
5. LEDs (4 required)
6. 220 Ohm Resistors (4 required)
7. Connecting Jumper Wires
8. 5V 2A Power Adapter
9. An active 2.4 Ghz Authentication less or WPA/WPA2 wifi connection

ESP32 DEVKIT V1 Wiring Connections:
  IR Sensors (Slots): Pins 32, 33, 25, 26
  LEDs (Slots): Pins 19, 18, 5, 17 (Along with Resistor for each in series with the LED)
  IR Sensor (Gate): Pin 27.
  Servo Motor (Gate): Pin 16.
  OLED Display: Standard I2C pins (SDA = 21, SCL = 22).

NOTE: Its important to not connect the servo directly to the ESP32's 3.3V Out or the VIN pin because it may fry the board.
Try to connect it directly to the power source. The rest can be connected directly to the board's power including OLED connection to the 3.3V out pin.

Required Libraries:
  ESP32Servo by Kevin Harrington (Standard Servo.h can be glitchy on ESP32).
  Adafruit SSD1306 (For the OLED).
  Adafruit GFX Library (Dependency for the OLED).
